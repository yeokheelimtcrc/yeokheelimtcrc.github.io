前言：潮音不息，薪火相传

Introduction: Echoes of Teochew Opera, a legacy unbroken

南华文化节展览以“传承与创新”为主题，呈现潮剧在新加坡近两百年的发展历程。从十九世纪潮人“过番”南来，潮剧在庙会街戏与戏园中扎根，到二十世纪的黄金年代、银幕热潮与电视转型，再到新时代的跨界合作与国际交流，潮剧始终与社会脉动紧密相连，见证了移民群体的奋斗与文化坚守。


展览内容涵盖：
1. 潮剧简史：梳理潮剧在狮城的历史流变；
2. 南华潮剧社：回顾自1963年成立以来的传承与发展；
3. 当代创新剧目：展示《情断昆吾剑》《典妻》等代表性作品；
4. 研究中心馆藏：展现逾万册珍贵文献，支撑潮学研究；
5. 口述史档案：保存艺人、社员的第一手记忆与故事。（待定）


通过文献、影像、实物与互动展板，本展览不仅回顾潮剧在新加坡的历史与成就，更强调其在当代社会的文化价值与未来可能。潮剧既是潮人乡愁的寄托，也是新加坡多元文化的重要组成部分。

The Nam Hwa Cultural Festival Exhibition, themed “Inheritance and Innovation”, presents nearly two centuries of Teochew Opera’s development in Singapore. From the 19th‑century Teochew migrants who brought opera to temple fairs and street stages, through the golden age, the silver screen craze, and television adaptations of the 20th century, to today’s cross‑disciplinary collaborations and international exchanges, Teochew Opera has remained closely intertwined with the rhythms of society, embodying both resilience and cultural continuity.


The exhibition highlights:
1. A Brief History of Teochew Opera in Singapore – tracing its historical evolution;
2. Nam Hwa Opera – reviewing the society’s journey since its founding in 1963;
3. Contemporary Productions – showcasing representative works such as The Severing Sword and Pawned Wife;
4. Research Center Collections – presenting over 10,000 rare volumes supporting Teochew studies;
5. Oral History Archives – preserving first‑hand memories and stories of artists and community members. (TBC)


Through documents, images, artifacts, and interactive displays, the exhibition not only revisits Teochew Opera’s history and achievements in Singapore, but also emphasizes its cultural value and future vitality. Teochew Opera is both a vessel of nostalgia for the Teochew diaspora and an integral part of Singapore’s multicultural identity.

南华潮剧社历史

History of Nam Hwa Opera

1963年，新加坡一群热爱潮剧的同好者成立了“南华儒剧社”，以弘扬潮人优秀传统文化为宗旨。2018年更名为“南华潮剧社”，标志着其在新加坡潮剧舞台上的新起点。

In 1963, a group of Teochew opera enthusiasts in Singapore founded the Nam Hwa Amateur Musical and Dramatic Association. In 2018, it was renamed Nam Hwa Opera, marking a new chapter in its cultural mission.

其他剧种在新加坡

Other Opera Forms in Singapore

除了潮剧，新加坡还活跃着越剧、婺剧、歌仔戏等剧种，展现了多元戏曲文化的交流与融合。

Besides Teochew Opera, Singapore also hosts Yue Opera, Wu Opera, and Hokkien Opera, reflecting the diversity and cultural exchange of Chinese theatrical traditions.
